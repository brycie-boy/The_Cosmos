## Right Speech  #🧠 

Right Speech, part of [[The Nobel Eightfold Path]], consists of abstaining from:

- lying
- malicous speech
- harsh speech
- idle chatter

___

##### References

- Saṃyutta Nikāya 45:8; V 8–10
