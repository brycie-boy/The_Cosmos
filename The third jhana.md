## The third jhana  #🧠 

The third jhana, one of [[The jhanas]], consists of the fading away of rapture and happiness from [[The first jhana]] and [[The second jhana]] from which comes a feeling of equanimity.

___

##### References

- Saṃyutta Nikāya 45:8; V 8–10
